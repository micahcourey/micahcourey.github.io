About Me

Three years ago I made a decision to turn my life long interest and fascination in technology into a career. At that time I was working as a restaurant Assistant Manager at The Trailhead Restaurant in Estes Park, CO. I decided that to achieve my new found goals as efficiently as possible I should go back to school full time.

In November of 2012 I turned in a one month notice at my job and moved back home to Georgia. To save money I stayed with my parents while attending school full time at Chattahoochee Technical College majoring in Computer Information Systems Technology. I enjoyed my classes and I learned a lot while earning my degree and in the process I found my biggest passion in technology is in programming and development.

After I graduated with an Associate's Degree I decided that instead of looking for a job in IT I would continue my education to gain all the skills I would need to become a Web Developer. After many hours of researching schools and programs all across the country I came to the conclusion that Epicodus in the beautiful city of Portland, OR was the best place for me. Although I’m very good at learning on my own by taking advantage of all the great online learning resources, I felt that a program like epicodus would prepare me for all of the non technical skills I wanted to improve on such as working on team projects and pair programming.

In July of 2015 I packed my car to the ceiling and started my 37 hour drive across the country. While I made it across the country in one piece, unfortunately my cars engine did not. I have learned so many new things at my time at Epicodus both technical and nontechnical. I’m really looking forward to starting my career in Web Development.
